\# Privacy Policy for Chinese Number Trainer



Last updated: June 2026



Chinese Number Trainer ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.



\## Information We Collect



The App does \*\*not collect, store, or transmit personal information\*\*.



The App does not require users to create an account or provide personal data.



\## Advertising



The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.



These services may collect certain information automatically, such as:



\* Device information

\* Advertising identifiers

\* Interaction with ads



This information is collected and processed by the advertising provider according to their own privacy policies.



For more information, please review Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy



\## Analytics



The App may use basic analytics tools to understand how the app is used, such as session duration or feature usage. This helps improve the app experience.



These analytics tools may collect anonymized information such as:



\* Device type

\* App usage statistics

\* Crash reports



No personally identifiable information is collected.



\## Children's Privacy



The App is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.



If you believe that personal information has been collected from a child, please contact us so that it can be removed.
